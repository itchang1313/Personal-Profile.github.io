Chicken Afritada

  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 kilo chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 pack (200-gram) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 large potatoes, quartered
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • 1 large red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Saute the onion and garlic in a preheated pan with cooking oil.
  2. Stir in the chicken until the color turns light brown.
  3. Pour-in tomato sauce and water then simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.
  4. Add the potato and carrots then cook for 7 minutes or until the vegetables are soft.
  5. Put-in the bell pepper slices. Season with salt, sugar, and ground black pepper then cook for a few minutes. Serve hot.
